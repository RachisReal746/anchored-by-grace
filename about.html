Founder's Story

Anchored by Grace is a collaboration grown from personal, professional and spiritual experience. I believe recovery is more than just kicking the addiction — it's about healing, rebuilding, and finding strength in your story and making the positive connections that bring you joy.

Both lived experience and the people I have supported has confirmed that successful recovery needs a meaningful connection to walk alongside you, hold hope when you can't, and guide the way when you're lost. With the right tools and unwavering support, you can and you will thrive, because this journey wasn't meant to be faced alone.

Anchored by Grace was born from my own lived experience with addiction and recovery. I know firsthand the pain, the isolation, and the overwhelming struggle to keep going when there's nothing left to fight for. But I also know what it's like to reach the other side — to discover hope, healing, and a renewed sense of purpose.

My own recovery journey taught me that with the right connections, community, and faith, shifting your focus on the broken parts of us, it is not only possible — it's powerful when you restore the you that you thought was gone.

That's why I created Anchored by Grace — to provide the safe, supportive anchor of connection that I once needed. Through our virtual platform, you have 24/7 access to:

  • Counselling chatbot, Gracie
  • Online appointment counselling service
  • Self-help tools and resources
  • Personal blog — the raw truth
  • Free downloadable Gracie app with game-changing, unique features
  • The opportunity to take control and create a recovery experience that fits your goals

The Launch of Gracie, a uniquely designed downloadable app for mobiles, will ensure you always stay anchored to hope and support. With innovative features and tools accessible exclusively through Gracie, that uncovers your own strength and intuition, I'm confident you will achieve your goals.

Whether you're exploring what recovery looks like for you, thinking of making a change, just starting out in your new life, or looking for ongoing support to stay on track, we're here to meet you wherever you are, without judgment.

You are not broken. You are becoming. And who you are becoming is amazing. We're here to help you stay anchored in hope, grace, and lasting change.

God Bless, Rachael x
